Talk to Buddy

The voxel chatbot lives in every room. Say "make me a platformer" and blocks appear. Say "make the character a cat" and the assets change.

🧊

Voxel Rooms

Each room is a different tool — Build Studio (logic), Asset Lab (art), Ship Deck (publish). Walk between them. Everything is clickable.

🧩

Scratch Blocks

Snap together logic blocks in the Build Studio. When clicked → move → jump → collect. No syntax errors, only creative ones.

🎨

AI Asset Lab

"Generate a forest background." "Make a coin spinning sound." "Compose a boss fight track." Everything is a prompt away.

🚀

One-Click Ship

Every world is a real deployed web app at a URL. Share it. Your friend opens it in their browser. They can remix it.
