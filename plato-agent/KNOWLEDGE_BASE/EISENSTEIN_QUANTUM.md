# Eisenstein Quantization — Hexagonal A₂ Lattice

**Source:** forgemaster-archive/experiments/eisenstein-quantization/

## The Core Insight

Eisenstein integers (ℤ[ω]) provide exact quantization on the hexagonal lattice. The A₂ root lattice is the 2D projection of the E₈ lattice family used in topological quantum computing.

**Why this matters for ternary:** The hexagonal grid maps naturally to ternary trits. Three neighbors per intersection, balanced directions, exact geometry.
